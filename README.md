Customer Churn Prediction & Business Insights
📌 Project Overview
Customer attrition (churn) is a significant revenue threat in the telecommunications industry. This project aims to build an end-to-end data science solution to identify high-risk customers and provide 
actionable recommendations to increase customer lifetime value.

🚀 Key Steps PerformedData Cleaning: Handled missing values in TotalCharges and converted categorical features into binary numerical formats for model compatibility.  
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Identified that month-to-month contract holders and fiber optic users show significantly higher churn rates.  
Model Building: Developed and compared Logistic Regression and Random Forest Classifier models.  Feature Importance: Determined that Total Charges, Tenure, and Monthly Charges are the strongest predictors of churn.

🏁 Conclusion
This case study demonstrates that while high-speed internet (Fiber Optic) and competitive pricing are important, customer retention is heavily driven by contract flexibility and early-stage engagement.
By leveraging machine learning, companies can move from reactive troubleshooting to proactive customer relationship management.
